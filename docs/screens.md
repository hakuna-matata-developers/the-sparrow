This document defines all user-facing screens and how they are accessed.
Navigation is primarily drawer-based, with quick actions on Home.

---

## 1. Splash Screen
- App logo
- Tagline (Hakuna Matata experience)
- Short loading animation

---

## 2. Home Screen
- Welcome message
- Quick cards:
  - Animals
  - Parks
  - Friends of Nature (Daily)
- Daily fun fact or joke
- Highlights (Animal of the Day / Park Highlight)

---

## 3. Navigation Drawer (Main Menu)
Accessible from Home and most screens.

Items:
- Home
- Animals
- Birds
- Parks
- Friends of Nature
- Quiz & Games
- Safari Journal
- RAFIKI Chatbot
- Offline Downloads
- Profile
- Settings
- About H.MTALII / THE SPARROW

---

## 5. Animal Detail Screen
- Animal image
- Name & scientific name
- Habitat
- Parks & countries
- Conservation status
- Fun facts
- Cultural beliefs / myths

---

## 6. Birds Screen (Module)
- African birds list
- Filters (resident / migratory)
- Search
- Entry point to bird details

---

## 7. Bird Detail Screen
- Bird image
- Name & scientific name
- Bird call (play)
- Habitat
- Migratory status & map
- Subspecies
- Fun facts

---

## 8. Parks Screen
- Nearby parks (GPS-based)
- Option to explore other regions
- Park cards with brief info

---

## 9. Park Detail Screen
- Park overview
- Wildlife highlights
- Offline map & trails
- Points of interest
- Tips and guidelines

---

## 10. Friends of Nature (Daily Page)
- Animal of the day
- Conservation messages
- Wildlife jokes
- Community or park highlights
- Positive nature actions

This page is updated regularly and gives the app a living heartbeat.

---

## 11. Quiz & Games Screen
- Wildlife quizzes
- Bird quizzes
- Park-based quizzes
- Difficulty progression
- Offline support

---

## 12. RAFIKI Chatbot Screen
- Chat interface
- Ask wildlife questions
- Get fun facts and jokes
- App guidance and help
- Friendly, nature-inspired personality

---

## 13. Safari Journal Screen
- List of trips or dates
- Add:
  - Notes
  - Photos
  - Voice recordings
- Works offline

---

## 14. Offline Downloads Screen
- Download content by region
- Storage usage indicator
- Manage downloaded data

---

## 15. Profile Screen
- Profile photo
- Name and role (tourist, guide, student)
- Language preference
- Favorites & bookmarks
- Quiz progress

---

## 16. Settings Screen
- Language
- Sound on/off
- Theme (auto / light / dark)
- Data & storage preferences
