H.MTALII, now known as THE SPARROW, is a safari companion and wildlife
learning application built for Africa.

The app is designed to work offline, support tour guides, educate users,

## 1. Core Purpose

- Help users explore African wildlife and safari parks
- Support tour guides and their guests
- Educate through fun, stories, jokes, and facts
- Encourage conservation and love for nature
- Work reliably in low-connectivity environments

---

## 2. Animals Page (Core Feature)

- Includes ALL animals found in Africa
- Categories:
  - Mammals
  - Birds
  - Reptiles
  - Amphibians
  - Fish & marine life
- Each animal has:
  - Name & scientific name
  - Medium-sized image (offline friendly)
  - Habitat
  - Parks and countries found
  - Endangered / conservation status
  - Fun facts
  - Cultural beliefs or myths
- Priority animals can be highlighted (e.g. whale üêã)

## 4. Safari Parks & Locations

- Nearby parks using GPS

- Trails and points of interest

- Conservation messages
- Wildlife jokes
